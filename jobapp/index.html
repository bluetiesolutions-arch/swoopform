About the role
We're looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our growing product team and help shape the future of how our customers interact with our platform. You'll work closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to design intuitive and delightful user experiences — from research and discovery through to high-fidelity prototypes and production assets.
What you'll do
  • Lead end-to-end design for 1–2 product areas
  • Conduct user research and usability testing
  • Create wireframes, user flows, and high-fidelity prototypes
  • Collaborate with engineers to ensure design quality
  • Maintain and evolve our design system
  • Mentor junior designers on the team
Requirements
  • 2–4 years of product design experience
  • Strong proficiency in Figma
  • Experience with user research methods
  • A strong portfolio showing end-to-end design work
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
Perks & Benefits
  • Flexible working hours & hybrid setup
  • Health insurance for you + family
  • ₹50,000 annual learning budget
  • Annual team offsite
  • MacBook Pro + design tools provided
About Acme Corp
Acme Corp builds productivity software for distributed teams. We're trusted by 3,000+ companies across 40 countries. Our product team is 18 people strong and growing fast.
