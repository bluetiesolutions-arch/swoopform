FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is SwoopForm really free? ▼
Yes! The Free plan is free forever — no credit card required. You get 3 active surveys with 100 responses each. Upgrade only when you need more.
How does Google Sheets sync work? ▼
When you publish a survey, SwoopForm automatically creates a Google Sheet in your Drive. Every new response is instantly added as a new row — no manual exports needed.
What is logic branching? ▼
Logic branching lets you show or hide questions based on previous answers. For example: if someone answers "No" to a question, you can skip 3 questions and jump straight to the next relevant one.
Can students and NGOs use SwoopForm for free? ▼
Yes! Verified students and NGOs get unlimited surveys and responses completely free. Submit your verification from Settings and our team will review within 24 hours.
What question types are available? ▼
SwoopForm supports 16+ question types including: Multiple choice, Checkboxes, Dropdown, Yes/No, Short text, Long text, Email, Phone, Number, Rating 1-5, Scale 1-10, Date, File URL, Pledge/Statement, and Quiz MCQ.
Can I add a prize to my survey? ▼
Yes! You can add a prize badge (cash, voucher, gift, or other) to boost response rates. Choose random draw, best answer, or first N respondents as the selection method.
Is my data safe? ▼
All data is stored securely on Google Firebase with enterprise-grade security. Your responses are private by default and only accessible to you. We never sell your data.
