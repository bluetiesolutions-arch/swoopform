Frequently asked questions
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ▼
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens when I hit my response limit? ▼
Your forms will stop accepting new responses until the next month or until you upgrade. Existing responses are always safe and accessible.
How does student/educator/NGO verification work? ▼
After signing up, apply for free access through your account settings. Our team reviews your application within 2-3 working days. Once approved, access is valid for 1 year. You'll need to re-verify annually — this helps ensure our free tier stays available for those who need it most.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund.
Is there a free trial on paid plans? ▼
The Free plan is effectively a permanent trial — use SwoopForm with no time limit. Upgrade when you need more forms, responses or advanced features.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. For Business plans, we can arrange invoice-based billing on request.
