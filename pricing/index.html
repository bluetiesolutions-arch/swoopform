Frequently asked questions
Can I try paid features before upgrading?▼
Yes! All new accounts start on the Free plan with full access. When you're ready to unlock more responses, remove branding, or access Live Quiz and PulseCheck, you can upgrade in Settings.
How does the student / NGO verification work?▼
Go to Settings → Verification, choose your account type, upload a public link to your document (Google Drive, Dropbox) and submit. We review within 24–48 hours and email you the result.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?▼
Yes. You can upgrade any time — your plan takes effect immediately. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Contact us at support@swoopform.com to change your plan.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking and wallets (Razorpay). All payments are secure and encrypted.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?▼
Your data is always safe. If you exceed Free plan limits after downgrading, existing surveys remain accessible in read-only mode. You won't lose any responses — you just won't be able to create new surveys above the limit.
Do you offer refunds?▼
Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Contact support@swoopform.com within 7 days of your payment.
