Pricing FAQs
Is the free plan really free forever? ▼
Yes! The Free plan has no time limit and no credit card required. You get 3 surveys with 100 responses each, forever.
How do I get the free Student/Educator or NGO plan? ▼
Go to Settings → Account type → Select Student/Educator or NGO → Apply for verification. Upload your ID or proof of organisation. Our team reviews within 24 hours.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
Yes — we offer a 7-day refund policy on all paid plans. Email contact@swoopform.com within 7 days of payment.
Are there discounts for teams or institutions? ▼
Yes! For educational institutions or teams needing multiple accounts, we offer custom pricing. Email contact@swoopform.com for a quote.
