Common questions
How quickly do you reply?
We typically reply within 24 hours on weekdays. Complex issues may take up to 48 hours.
How do I get verified as a student or NGO?
Go to Settings → Account type → Select Student/Educator or NGO → Apply for verification. We review within 24 hours.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — we offer a 7-day refund policy on all paid plans. Just email us within 7 days of payment.
Do you offer discounts for bulk plans?
Yes! For teams or educational institutions needing multiple accounts, email us for custom pricing.
