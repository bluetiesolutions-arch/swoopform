SwoopForm was born out of frustration with clunky, outdated survey tools that get in the way of collecting great data.

Our story

Why we built SwoopForm

Every survey tool we tried felt like it was designed for a different era. Complicated interfaces, ugly forms, limited logic, and expensive plans that made no sense for students, researchers, and small teams.

We believed surveys could be beautiful, smart, and fast to build. That collecting responses should feel effortless — for both the creator and the respondent. That powerful features like logic branching, quiz scoring, and real-time analytics shouldn't be locked behind enterprise pricing.

So we built SwoopForm. A survey platform designed from the ground up to be simple, powerful, and accessible to everyone — from a student running a research project to a business collecting customer feedback at scale.

Our mission

Democratising data collection

We believe great decisions start with great data. And great data starts with surveys that people actually want to fill out.

Our mission is to make professional-grade survey tools accessible to everyone — students, educators, NGOs, researchers, and businesses of all sizes. That's why we offer a genuinely free plan, and free forever plans for verified students, educators and non-profits.
